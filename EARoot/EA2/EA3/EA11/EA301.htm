<b> explanation: </b> <br/> a {trade name} is a name including a {company} or a {location} of a {company} acts. <br/> <br/> a {company} may have several {trade names}. The {location} has at least one, or more, {handelsna (a) m (s)} with which it comes out. <br/> <br/> with private law {legal persons}, the statutory name is always one of the {trade names} of the corresponding {enterprise}. <br/> <br/> the {trade names} of the {enterprise} are a list of all {trade names} of all {branches}. If a {trade name} prevents double, it will only be shown once. <br/> <br/>
