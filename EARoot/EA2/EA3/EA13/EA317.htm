Authentic = no

Legal text = Article 51 paragraph 3 of HRB
Notes: Indicates whether the address, based on a shielding request, is shielded or not. <br/><br/>an address of the branch or legal entity can be shielded. An address of the branch or legal entity can be viewed publicly, unless it is shielded. <br/><br/>a residential address of a natural person cannot be shielded, because it is already implicitly shielded on the basis of hrb article 51, paragraph 1. A residential address cannot be viewed publicly, with the exception of the 'custodian of books and documents' official on the basis of hrb article 51, paragraph 2. Residential addresses can be viewed by customers mentioned in hrb article 51, paragraph 1. <br/><br/>please note that the indicator for a residential address of a natural person is always set to "no", because the residential address cannot be shielded on the basis of a shielding request. The home address does enjoy legal shielding. <br/><br/>
