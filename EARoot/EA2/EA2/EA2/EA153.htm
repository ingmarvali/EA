<b>definition: </b><br/>a {social activity} is the activity of a {natural person} or {non-natural person}. <br/><br/><b>explanation: </b><br/>the {social activity} is the sum total of all activities carried out by a {natural person} or a {non-natural person}. A {social activity} can also appear as {enterprise}. <br/><br/><b>legislation: </b><br/>article 13 of the criminal code. <br/>
