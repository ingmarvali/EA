<b>definition (system catalogue): </b><br/>a {legal person} is a legal subject made possible by law, which is the bearer of rights and obligations. <br/><br/><b>explanation: </b><br/>legal entities are exhaustively included in the legislation (in particular in the civil code). <br/><br/><b>legislation: </b><br/>articles 2: 1 to 2: 3 of the dutch civil code<br/>articles 1 and 6 of the hrw<br/>article 12 of the hrw-private<br/> <i>legal entities: </i><br/>article 22 of the hrb<br/>article 23 of the hrb<br/>article 28 of the hrb<br/>article 29 of the hrb<br/>article 30 of the hrb-other<br/> <i>legal entities under private law: </i><br/>article 32 of the <i>hrb-kerkgenootschap</i><br/><br/> article 31 of the <i>hrb-public-law</i><br/> legal entity: <br/>articles 33 and 34 of the hrb<br/><br/><b>population description: </b><br/>the collection of legal entities consists of legal entities under private law, legal entities under public law and religious denominations. <br/>
